## Basics

A drawer can be configured to appear from any direction using the `direction` prop.

-   Left and right drawers will size to their content width
-   Top and bottom drawers will size to their content height

#### Scrolling

Overflow is set to auto depening on direction:

-   Left and right have `overflow-y: auto`
-   Top and Bottom have `overflow-x: auto`
