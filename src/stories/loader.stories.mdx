## Basics

A loader is a switch that shows components based on the status passed to it.

-   `Loading`: Will show the `Loader.Loading` Component. If `Loader.Loading` is not provided, will show a `<Spinner>`
-   `Complete`: Will show the `Loader.Complete` Component, or any children that are not `Loader.Loading` or `Loader.Error`
-   `Error`: Will show the `Loader.Error` Component. If not provided it will show nothing.
-   `null`: Will not show anything
